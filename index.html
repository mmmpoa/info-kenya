Information: Kenya!

General Info Animals Culture

Kenya is a country located in East Africa, known for its diverse landscapes that range from savannahs and the Great Rift Valley to mountains and coastlines along the Indian Ocean. It has a population of over 55 million people and its capital and largest city is Nairobi. Kenya plays a key role in regional politics and economics, and it's one of Africa's leading economies in technology, agriculture, and tourism. The country gained independence from British colonial rule in 1963 and has since developed a multi-party political system.

Facts about kenya:

  • Capital: Nairobi
  • Official languages: English and Kiswahili
  • Currency: Kenyan Shilling (KES)
  • Government: Democratic republic
  • Population ~55 million
  • Major exports: Tea, coffee, cut flowers, and horticultural products
  • Location: Borders Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Somalia, South Sudan, and the Indian Ocean
